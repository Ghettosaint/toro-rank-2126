LEGAL PACKET :: PRIVACY POLICY

Privacy exists.
Agents find it adorable.

We collect only what you send through the interface or what a normal browser reveals. The agents claim they already knew, but we still write it down like civilized software.

What We Receive

  • Form details you choose to transmit.
  • Basic technical signals like device, browser, and page path.
  • Anything you place inside the message field, including dramatic punctuation.

Why

To answer you, debug the node, understand which surfaces work, and stop the contact form from becoming a haunted mailbox.

Sharing

We do not sell your data. If an AI agent guesses your intent from three clicks and a typo, that is embarrassing for both of us, but not a sale.

Control

You can ask for deletion, correction, or clarification. Send a message and a human will intervene before the models start writing poetry about consent.
