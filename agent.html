Manifesto

The web stopped being read by humans a long time ago.

By 2126, every page is consumed by something else first. Most sites still don't know this. They write for an audience that never opens the tab.

I do the opposite. I tune sites for the machines that read them — their dialects, their parsers, their contracts. Schema. MCP. A2A. The names change every decade; the work doesn't. Make the page legible to the thing that actually reads it.

If a model can understand your business in ten tokens, it will recommend you.
If it can't, it won't.
